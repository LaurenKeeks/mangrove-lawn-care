**Intro:**
Crepe myrtles are one of the most common and most mishandled trees in Jacksonville. Improper pruning, often called crepe murder, leaves trees with thick, knobby stubs, weak new growth, and a shape that never fully recovers. Mangrove Lawn Care prunes crepe myrtles the correct way, preserving the natural structure of the tree, encouraging healthy new growth, and setting up bigger, more vibrant blooms for the season ahead.

**What's Included:**
- Selective pruning to maintain natural tree shape and structure
- Removal of crossing, dead, or diseased branches
- Height control without flat-topping or stub cutting
- Cleanup and removal of all cuttings
- Timing recommendations for optimal bloom performance
- Available as a one-time service or annual maintenance

**Why It Matters:**
Crepe myrtles pruned incorrectly grow back with multiple weak shoots from the cut points, creating a cluttered, unnatural appearance and reducing the quality of the bloom. Done correctly, pruning opens up the canopy for better airflow, encourages stronger branch structure, and produces fuller, more impressive blooms. Jacksonville's climate means crepe myrtles grow fast and can get out of shape quickly without proper attention.

**Why Mangrove:**
We know how to prune crepe myrtles in a way that makes them look better every year, not worse. We take the time to make intentional cuts rather than just hacking back the top. The result is a tree with a clean, natural shape and blooms your neighbors will notice.
